# HR Attrition Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview
Analyzed 1,000 employees to identify key drivers of employee turnover and retention risk.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Employees**: 1,000
- **Attrition Count**: 237
- **Attrition Rate**: 16.12%
- **Avg Monthly Income**: $6.50K
- **Avg Years at Company**: 7

## Key Insights
1. **Sales is the problem**: Sales Reps are leaving at 39.8%. That's 2.5x higher than the company average of 16.12%
2. **Overtime + Unhappy = People Quit**: Staff doing overtime with low job satisfaction leave at 35.71%. OT alone isn't it — it's OT + low satisfaction
