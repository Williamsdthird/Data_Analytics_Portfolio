# Telco Customer Churn Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview
Analyzed ~7,000 customers to identify drivers of customer churn and retention opportunities for a telecom company.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Customers**: 7,000
- **Churn Rate**: 26.58%
- **Retention Rate**: 73.42%
- **Average Monthly Charge**: $64.80

## Key Insights
1. **Contract Type is the biggest churn driver**: Month-to-month customers have a 43% churn rate vs 3% for 2-year contracts. Customers want commitment incentives
2. **Churn is highest in the first year**: Customers with 0-12 months tenure churn at 48%. The rate drops steadily to 10% for customers with 49+ months. Early engagement programs could reduce overall churn
3. **Payment method correlates with churn risk**: Electronic check is the most used payment method at 33.63% and is linked to higher churn. Automatic payment methods like credit card lower churn because they reduce friction
