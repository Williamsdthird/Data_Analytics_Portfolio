# Sales Dashboard 2025 | Power BI

## Overview
Analyzed 4 years of sales data (2009-2012) totaling $1.65M in revenue across 872 orders to identify top products, regions, and profit drivers.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Sales**: $1.65M
- **Total Profit**: $198.43K
- **Profit Margin**: 11.99%
- **Total Orders**: 872

## Key Insights - Overview
1. **Product**: Technology makes the most sales with 5000K+ in sales
2. **Province**: Ontario drives the most revenue at $0.47M
3. **Margin Alert**: Furniture brings the most profit despite lower sales volume

## Key Insights - Product Analysis
1. **Technology = 37% of sales**: Highest revenue category
2. **Furniture loss**: Furniture lost $16K despite $0.59M in sales due to low margin products
3. **Top 10 Focus**: Focus on top 5 profitable products to grow overall profit

## Key Insights - Customers & Regions
1. **Corporate segment #1**: $500K+ in sales
2. **Volume vs Value**: Darren Budd is the top sales maker, but some low-margin customers hurt profit
3. **Regional Gap**: Nunavut has the lowest sales at $0.02M

Power BI, Sales Analytics, Profitability Analysis, DAX, Regional Analysis, Product Performance
