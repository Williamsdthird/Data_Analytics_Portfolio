# Customer ROI Dashboard - Online Retail | Power BI

## Overview
Analyzed $8.91M in revenue across 397,884 customers from multiple countries to identify high-value customers and revenue drivers.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: $8.91M
- **Total Customers**: 397,884
- **Total Orders**: 18,532
- **Average Order Value**: $480.87
- **Avg Recency**: 5,335 days

## Key Insights
1. **1% Rule**: 1% of customers drive 60%+ of total revenue. Customer 14646 alone is worth $280K
2. **UK Dominance**: United Kingdom is the best market, driving 82% of total revenue with $7.3M
3. **Top Countries**: Netherlands, EIRE, and Germany are the next biggest markets after UK
4. **VIP Focus**: Top 5 customers generate $947K combined. Retention should focus here
