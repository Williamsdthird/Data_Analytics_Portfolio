# Customer ROI Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview
Analyzed $8.91M in revenue across 397,884 customers to identify top-value customers and retention opportunities.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: $8.91M
- **Total Customers**: 397,884  
- **Total Orders**: 18,532

## Key Insights
1. **1% Rule**: 1% of customers drive 60%+ of total revenue. Customer 14646 alone = $280K
2. **UK Dominance**: United Kingdom accounts for 82% of all revenue
3. **Opportunity**: Focus retention on top 1% to protect majority of revenue

Power BI, DAX, Customer Segmentation, Revenue Analysis, Data Visualization
